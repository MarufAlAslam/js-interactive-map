üìã Important Notes for SVG Configuration

  • Grey Color Required: All clickable regions must have fill color rgb(199, 200, 202) or #C7C8CA
  • Group Naming: Thick dark grey borders (groups) need IDs matching the region names above
  • Not Individual States: Don't make every state clickable individually - group them according to the regions above
  • Logo Files: All 12 company logos are in img/logos/ directory

‚ö†Ô∏è To Make Bottom Regions (Mexico/South America) Work:

  • In your SVG editor, group all Mexico shapes and name the group "Mexico"
  • Group all South America shapes and name the group "SouthAmerica" or "South America"
  • Ensure these groups have the grey border color #C7C8CA
  • Open browser console (F12) and click regions to see detection logs

Testing Instructions

  1. Open index.html in your web browser
  2. Open the browser console (F12 or Right-click ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí Console)
  3. Hover over regions to see them turn red
  4. Click on each region listed above
  5. Console will show which region was detected or why it failed
  6. Verify the correct company popup appears
